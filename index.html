About Me

Namaste! I am Aaditya Prakash Kattekola and I like to learn about how stuff works in order to re-implement and simulate them. Driven by this desire, I had the urge to figure out how entities work, be it animate or inanimate. I also delved into game development in the past, which provided me with a sandbox to recreate thinking and where I developed intelligent humanoids, computer players etc.

I wish to pursue my higher studies in control systems as it is fundamentally mathematical and quite multidisciplinary. My love for mathematics arose from the fact that no matter how complex the nature of an object is, in the end, it can almost always be encapsulated by a mathematical equation.

I read research papers, create interactive environments, listen to classical music, drink coffee and play chess in my free time. Other than that I conduct workshops, hackathons, attend conferences and run through lectures & courses. All in all, I like to keep myself busy with exploring new things that keep the gears in my brain turning and as a consequence, average on 4 hours of sleep.
